Our Company


We are a startup focused on helping software engineers get better in technical skills, soft skills, and career skills in order to get a job they love in a place they love.

Founding Story

In 2006, when I lived in Kyrgyzstan I got an email from a Google recruiter. It was so unbelievable, I thought it was a scam.

I found it difficult to prepare and failed to get the job.

I didn't give up. I worked on myself for many months after that and from the second try I managed to get the job!

I relocated to Ireland and was now surrounded by so many amazing people and the environment helped me prosper.

Now, we want to help millions of people around the world believe in themselves, dream big, and achieve those dreams by working together in a community with mentors rather than applying alone.

- Tilek Mamutov

Our Startup

We started a startup instead of a typical company because we were inspired by such technology businesses as Airbnb, Google, Spotify, Stripe, Twitter, Tesla, and SpaceX.

We want to provide high-quality services for everyone we can around the world for as long as possible.

We decided to get investment and mentorship from some of the world's best investors to achieve that goal and achieve it as as soon as healthily possible.
