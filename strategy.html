Career Strategy Sessions


We currently offer one-on-one career guidance sessions with our founder to help you navigate your software engineering career path more effectively.


What to Expect


  • Personalized 45-minute session with Tilek Mamutov
  • Comprehensive analysis of your current career position
  • Industry insights from our experience with hundreds of engineers
  • Clear action plan tailored to your goals

Who Is It For?


It is ideal for experienced software engineers in one of the following situations:

  • You are considering a transition to big tech companies
  • You are considering a transition to startups
  • You are considering a relocation to Europe, US, or elsewhere
  • You would like to get more clarity for your career direction
You can be any type of software engineer - backend, frontend, full-stack, mobile, or AI.


Session Structure


  • Current Position Assessment (about 25 minutes)
    • Reviewing of your experience, skills, and job application history
    • Clarifying your career goals and motivations
    • Understanding your strengths and weaknesses
    • Understanding your current challenges and opportunities
    • Analysis of your market position
    • Identifying potential career paths

  • Action Plan Development (about 20 minutes)
    • Identifying an overall roadmap
    • Networking recommendations
    • Resource recommendations
    • Specific next steps
