Our Accelerator


Our career accelerator is an online interview preparation program for software engineers.

The program is meant to give the most important information, skills, and support to get great job offers within a few months and exclude everything that's not necessary.

It works best at succeeding in interviews at Google, Meta, Amazon, and Uber, but our graduates also get offers at Apple, DeepMind, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Palantir, Reddit, Snap, Stripe, TikTok, and other such leading tech companies.

Some of our graduates got offers and instead work at trading companies like Jane Street.
You can view our results to learn more about where our graduates are working.


What's Included

  • Data structures & algorithms interview preparation
  • Systems design interview preparation
  • Behavioral interview preparation
  • Resume preparation
  • Job application support & referrals
  • Recruiter communication support
  • Salary negotiation support

Program Length

  • Program before first job applications: 3 months
  • Program after first job applications: 2 months
  • Additional job application support is dependent on the payment option you choose

Program Format

  • You get access to our knowledge base
  • You get a week-by-week curriculum with 15+ hours worth of tasks
  • You get access to our gamified software that helps you progress faster
  • There are weekly progress check-in sessions with a batch lead to help you stay on track

Information Included in Our Knowledge Base

  • How interview processes at top tech companies work
  • Strategies of how to prepare for interviews
  • Strategies of how to perform at the interviews
  • Most important technical knowledge about data structures & algoriths
  • Most important technical knowledge about distributed systems design

Technical Interview Support with Dedicated Mentors

  • You get several seminars with mentors
  • You get several 1-1 practice interviews with your peers
  • You get several 1-1 practice & exam interviews with mentors helping you understand your strengths, areas for development, and readiness level

1-1 Behavioral Interviews, Resume & Referral Support

  • You get 1-1 behavioral interview coach support helping you improve your answers, your stories, and your communication skills over video
  • You get 1-1 resume coach support helping you make sure your resume looks best for top tech companies
  • You get referrals to potentially hundreds of top tech employers in our network, dependent on your performance within the program

Salary Negotiation Support

  • We help you coordinate your job applications for you to get multiple offers at the same time to make the salary negotiation easier
  • We have successful experience of negotiating more than 100 offers from big tech companies and will consult you in your communications with the recruiters

Long-Lasting Community

  • You become a part of a supportive community of software engineers that includes our successful graduates around the world, if you perform well during the program.



Pricing Options


Option 1 - Success-Based Fee

You pay 10% of base salary for 24 months, but only if you get the job you wanted.

Example:

If your salary is $100K per year, you pay $833.33 per month, totaling $20K overall. You fully keep your relocation bonus, sign-on bonus, annual bonuses, and all stock compensation.

Terms:

  • Only for backend or full-stack developers
  • Only for experienced developers (4+ years of experience)
  • A $1000 commitment fee
  • Need to pass our technical and communications vetting
  • Maximum chance of success


Option 2 - Monthly Subscription

You pay $1500 per month, cancel anytime, or stay as long as you want.

Terms:

  • Any software engineer can join
  • Any experience level is welcome
  • No commitment fee
  • No payments after getting a job
  • No need to pass our technical and communications vetting
  • Maximum flexibility in pricing


Option 3 - Mixed Payment

You pay $900 per month and % of future base salary

Percentage of base salary is based on the number of months you stay in the program.
For example: 1 month - 1% of base salary for 12 months.

Terms:

  • Any software engineer can join
  • Any experience level is welcome
  • No commitment fee
  • Reduced payments after getting a job
  • No need to pass our technical and communications vetting


Option 4 - Upfront Payment

You pay $10,000 upfront for 8 months of the program.

Terms:

  • Any software engineer can join
  • Any experience level is welcome
  • No commitment fee
  • No payments after getting a job
  • No need to pass our technical and communications vetting
  • Cheaper than $1500/month for 8 months
