Career Accelerator


Our flagship program is designed to help experienced software engineers prepare for and succeed in interviews at top-tier big tech companies.


Program Overview


The accelerator is an intensive, cohort-based program that combines structured learning, practice sessions, and personalized feedback. Working in small groups of up to 10 engineers, you'll develop the skills and confidence needed to excel in all types of interviews.


Who Should Apply


  • Backend or full-stack developers
  • (a) 4+ years of full-time professional experience and a Bachelor's degree
  • (b) 6+ years of full-time professional experience without a Bachelor's degree
  • Aiming to get into top big tech companies

Program Leads


  • Experienced group leader
  • Technical mentors with experience interviewing with and working at top big tech companies
  • Behavioral interview & resume mentor with experience helping tens of candidates pass interviews at top big tech companies

What You'll Learn


  • Data Structures & Algorithms
    • Advanced problem-solving techniques
    • Optimization strategies
    • Time and space complexity analysis
    • Pattern recognition in coding challenges

  • System Design
    • Distributed systems architecture
    • Scalability and performance optimization
    • Real-world case studies
    • Best practices and trade-offs

  • Interview Strategy
    • Communication and problem-solving framework
    • Whiteboarding techniques
    • Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
    • Mock interview practice
