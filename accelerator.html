Career Accelerator


Our flagship program is designed to help experienced software engineers prepare for and succeed in interviews at top-tier tech companies.


Program Overview


The Accelerator is an intensive, cohort-based program that combines structured learning, practice sessions, and personalized feedback. Working in small groups of up to 10 engineers, you'll develop the skills and confidence needed to excel in technical interviews.


Who Should Apply


  • Backend and full-stack developers with 4+ years of professional experience
  • Engineers with 6+ years of experience if no Bachelor's degree
  • Developers actively targeting positions at FAANG and similar top-tier companies
  • Engineers committed to intensive preparation and practice

What You'll Learn


  • Data Structures & Algorithms
    • Advanced problem-solving techniques
    • Optimization strategies
    • Time and space complexity analysis
    • Pattern recognition in coding challenges

  • System Design
    • Distributed systems architecture
    • Scalability and performance optimization
    • Real-world case studies
    • Best practices and trade-offs

  • Interview Strategy
    • Communication and problem-solving framework
    • Whiteboarding techniques
    • Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
    • Mock interview practice

Program Structure


  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Format: Live online sessions with recorded content
  • Group Size: Maximum 10 participants
  • Weekly Schedule:
    • 2 technical training sessions
    • 1 mock interview
    • 1 group problem-solving workshop
    • Office hours with mentors

Our Mentors


Learn from experienced engineers who have worked at and interviewed for top tech companies. Our mentor team includes current and former employees from Google, Meta, Amazon, and other leading tech companies.
