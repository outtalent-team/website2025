Welcome


We are a startup and a community focused on helping software engineers around the world improve their skills and get necessary knowledge to find and get a job in a company, a city, and a country where they can prosper.

We offer two types of services.

Accelerator

Its goal is to help outstanding software engineers aiming to get into top global tech companies. We do it in groups of up to 10 people. Many of those people become friends and become a part of our general community. Learn more

Career Strategy Sessions

These are 1-1 sessions with our founder Tilek Mamutov. Their goal is to help software engineers understand their position in the market, clarify their goals, and create a plan to improve their skills to eventually get their dream job in a dream location. Learn more
